1. Open XD

2. Choose file size with a W of 3120 and H of 2609 landscape mode document

3. Center Document

4. Create a circle using the circle tool that is the W 1020, and H 1300

5. Color fill it with #C2BBCF

6. Create two tiny circles that are W 184, and H 370,rotation 10, same color #C2BBCF, position them evenly on each side of the bigger circle, send forward both

7. Grab the rectangle tool and create a box with the W 255, and H 368, place this box at the center of the big circle at the bottom of the big circle, center bottom, colored #C2BBCF, send back

8. Next grab the triangle tool and create a triangle W 1275, H 690, rotation -180, colored #D6FF00, position at the bottom of the small rectangle in the middle

9. Grab the triangle tool again and create two triangles W 284, H 256, rotation 180, place them side by side with their corners touching, color them #FFFFFF

10. Grab the rectangle tool, W 405, H 1071, rotation -48, color #34DFFF, place two of these on either side of the triangle in the middle

11. Lastly grab the circle tool, W 1322, H 1164, color #ff0091

12. make sure the figure is cut off half way from the belly up to avoid showing empty space below.
